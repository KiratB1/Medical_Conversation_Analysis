**SOAP Note:**

**Subjective:**
The patient presents with a persistent cough for over two months, bringing up yellow, greenish sputum with a pink tinge, and experiencing night sweats. They report fevers, feeling hot, weight loss, and reduced appetite. The coughing occurs multiple times an hour and impacts sleep.

**Objective:**
- Patient is a smoker for 30 years, trying to cut down.
- Recent travel to India, staying in crowded conditions.
- No wheezing, chest pain, swelling, bowel movement changes, urinary problems, or dizziness/fainting.
- History of high blood pressure, type 2 diabetes, and high cholesterol, on medications for these conditions.
- Family history of heart attack in mother at 48.

**Assessment:**
1. Differential diagnosis includes:
   - Chronic bronchitis
   - Tuberculosis
   - Pneumonia
   - Lung cancer
   - Pulmonary fibrosis

**Plan:**
1. Order chest X-Ray to investigate for tuberculosis and pneumonia.
